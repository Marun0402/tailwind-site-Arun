Collaborate

With TeamSpaces, host your models and datasets with ease. Say goodbye to the hassle of cloud infrastructure maintenance and embrace a world of productivity and innovation.

One platform, infinite possibilities

Publish Effortlessly

Easily publish and upload your models through our user-friendly Rest API. Our platform takes care of the heavy lifting, converting uploaded models into easily searchable data microservices.

Enhanced API Security

Restrict your sensitive APIs restricted to specific teams or open them up for broader collaboration. The decision is in your hands, enabling you to achieve API security and seamless teamwork simultaneously.

Build API documentation on-the-go

Access the comprehensive API documentation, designed for seamless internal sharing. With real-time testing and code snippet tools, TeamSpaces ensures an intuitive experience for developers, making it easier than ever to design and integrate APIs.

Gateway to Vast Array of AI Capabilities

Discover ready-to-use APIs from our user-friendly platform and integrate them with your own custom models.

Connect, Monitor and Optimise with TeamSpaces

Access our extensive catalogue of public APIs on our platform to drive your innovation. As a TeamSpaces owner, you will enjoy a monthly credit allocation to call the APIs from our catalogue. If your usage exceeds the allocation, simply top up USD10.00 into your account to ensure uninterrupted access to the APIs your team needs.

Connect to Public APIs seamlessly

Leverage the power of the TeamSpaces platform to effortlessly connect to a wide range of public APIs from a centralised hub.

Comprehensive Usage Monitoring

With our built-in metering feature, make data-driven decisions to optimise your API utilisation and drive exponential business growth.

Optimised Usage

Enjoy a head start with 20 free credits worth of API calls on our platform upon signing up. Each additional API call is charged at an attractive tiered rate on a pay-per-use basis, giving you complete flexibility and control over your usage.

Tailored TeamSpaces Packages

TeamSpaces packages are yours to build and completely customisable. Craft a package that perfectly fits your requirements to drive success.

Affordable pricing

With affordable pricing starting as low as $200 and options for teams as small as 5 developers, we ensure that our offerings cater to organisations of all sizes.

Specialised packages

Specialised packages for eCommerce businesses and Enterprises are also available to maximise the value you derive from TeamSpaces and achieve optimal outcomes in your industry.

Who is TeamSpaces for?

TeamSpaces is purposefully designed to cater to a wide range of companies, offering a seamless collaboration platform for software development teams. Whether you’re a startup, enterprise, or seeking to securely share data with innovation partners, TeamSpaces has the solution you need to drive innovation and accelerate success.
